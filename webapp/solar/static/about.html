The GOSolar project

This web app is developed to help residents of Ontario wishing to install solar panels predict their energy output and savings. Calculations are based on the geographic location, panel size, roof pitch and orientation provided by the user. The model also takes into account yearly weather information for each specified location.

Some of the main assumptions used in the model are shown below:

- Estimate on the annual return is based on the current electricity price in Ontario (13.4 cents/kWh) with a projected and inflation adjusted 8.9% rate of increase

- Estimated cost is based on the US market and projected to Canadian dollars

- Break-even time is based on electricity prices projected over a 10 year period
